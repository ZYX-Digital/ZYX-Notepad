Welcome to ZYX Notepad!

ZYX Notepad is more than just a simple text editor; it's a powerful, persistent, and private workspace designed to live directly in your browser. Whether you're a developer needing a scratchpad, a writer drafting ideas, or just someone who needs to jot down notes quickly, ZYX Notepad is built to be your reliable digital companion.

Your Data & Privacy

Private and Persistent Storage

All your notes, tabs, and settings are saved only to your browser's localStorage. This means your data is private to you and never sent to a server.

Because your data is saved to a persistent file on your hard drive (managed by the browser), your work will survive a reboot of your computer. When you reopen this page, your notes will load exactly as you left them.

Important Warnings:
  • How Data Can Be Lost: Your data will be permanently lost if you:
    • Manually clear your browser's cache, cookies, or "site data."
    • Switch to a different browser (e.g., notes in Chrome won't appear in Firefox).
  • Private/Incognito Mode:
    • In a private window, your text will survive a page refresh or reload.
    • However, all data will be permanently erased the moment you close the private window or tab.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Split-Views: Organize your workspace with up to three split views. You can now rename any split by clicking its label.
  • Smart Tab Management: Create, rename, and close tabs freely. If you close a Split View, your open tabs are automatically migrated to the remaining view so you never lose your work.
  • Global Search: Instantly find what you're looking for. The search bar scans the content of all your tabs across all your splits and shows you exactly where your text is.
  • Built-in Tools Panel: Slide out the "Tools" panel (using the smart side-arrow) for quick access to:
    • A Standard Calculator (with full numpad support).
    • A Random Text Generator (for placeholders and passwords).
    • A Special Characters tool to easily find and copy unicode symbols.
    • Links to Full Calculator, DNS Info, and other external apps.
  • Offline-Ready: You can download a fully offline version of this page. Click More > Download Offline to save the application HTML file to your device.
  • Action History: Access the history panel using the side-arrow to view recent copy, cut, and delete actions, allowing you to retrieve text you thought was lost.
  • Full Customization:
    • Light & Dark Mode: Switch between a clean light theme or a sleek, eye-friendly dark mode.
    • Font Control: Adjust the font family and font size to find your perfect reading environment.
  • Notepad Utilities: Each editor panel is packed with features, including:
    • Live Word Count
    • Synchronized Line Numbers
    • A "Save as TXT" button to download your current tab.
    • A toggle for browser spellcheck.
  • Share Your Layout: The "Get Share URL" button creates a unique link that saves your current theme, font settings, and split-screen layout.

Responsive Mobile Layout

This application is fully responsive and designed to work great on mobile. On smaller screens, the layout adapts:

  • The main controls collapse into a convenient hamburger menu (☰).
  • The "More" menu and its options are flattened for easy, one-tap access.
  • Split-screen views will automatically stack vertically so you can scroll through your notes.

A Note on Tracking

Google Analytics

This page uses a standard Google Analytics tag to log a single "pageview" when the application first loads. This is done purely to monitor that the page is loading correctly.

Immediately after this single event (within 500 milliseconds), the script completely removes all Google Analytics code and deletes its data from the page. No further tracking of any kind occurs during your session.
